Bengal Tiger

Don't turn your back on a tiger! Natives often wear a mask of a face on the back of their heads to protect from attacks.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris
  • Average Size: Head to tail: 8 ft. & 400 lb.
  • Average Lifespan: 9 years
  • Habitat: In & around India

Bengals are the largest cat. They are nocturnal hunters. Between being hunted for trophies & Chinese medicine, as well as, destruction of their habitat, there are less than 2,500 Bengals left.
